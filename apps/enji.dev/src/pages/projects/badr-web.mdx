The BADR Web Redesign project aimed to revamp the website with a fresh, modern
UI based on Figma designs, while also improving its online visibility through
SEO enhancements. This redesign resulted in substantial business growth by
placing the website at the top of Google search results.

**Role**: Full Stack Web Developer(Remote)  
**Duration**: March - April 2023

---

## Key Features

- **Modern UI Design**: Transformed the website's interface with a sleek, intuitive design that enhances user experience.
- **SEO Optimization**: Achieved the top spot in Google search results, driving significant traffic and increasing business impact.
- **Effortless Content Management**: Built on **WordPress** and enhanced with the **Elementor** plugin for flexible content updates.

---

## Technologies Used

- **WordPress**: Platform for developing the website's structure and functionality.
- **Elementor Plugin**: Used to design and customize the UI elements based on the provided Figma designs.

---

## Impact

This redesign not only improved the aesthetic appeal of the BADR website but also increased its visibility, resulting in greater traffic and business growth.
